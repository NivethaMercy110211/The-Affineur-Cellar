Cellar Visits

Come to Beaune —
Walk the Cave

We welcome private guided visits to our cellar by appointment, Wednesday through Saturday. A visit to The Affineur Cellar includes a guided walk through all three cave zones, a tasting of four to six wheels at different stages of maturation, and a direct consultation with our affineur.

Visits are limited to a maximum of six people to preserve the intimacy of the experience and the quiet of the cave. We ask that visitors arrive wearing closed-toe shoes and without strong perfume, which can interfere with the delicate aromas of the ageing wheels.

I

Private Tour (up to 6)

1.5 hour guided walk through all cave zones, concluding with a tasting of key wheels. €45 per person. By appointment.

II

Affineur's Masterclass

Half-day intensive with Emile: guided cave walk, tasting workshop, and curated pairings. €120 per person. Max 4.

III

Trade & Press Visits

Complimentary tour for trade buyers, journalists, and food writers. Please bring credentials and book in advance.

Trade & Wholesale

Stocking Our

The Newsletter

Cellar Notes —
Dispatches from the Cave

Four times a year — at the change of each season — we write a letter from the cave. It contains the current seasonal release, an affineur's reflection on what is happening in the cellar, and occasional recipes and pairing ideas.

We have been sending Cellar Notes since 2009. No marketing language. No discounts. Just cheese, cave, and conversation.

Location

Finding Us in
Beaune, Burgundy

Our cellar is located in the medieval centre of Beaune, just off the main market square. The town is easily accessible by TGV from Paris (1h 40m) or Lyon (45 minutes).
