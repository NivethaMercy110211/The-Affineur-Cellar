Delivery

How We Pack
& Ship

Aged cheese is a living product. Our packaging is designed to preserve and protect it through every stage of its journey to your table.

❄

Temperature Controlled

All boxes ship in insulated wool packaging with a gel ice insert. Maintains 6–10°C for up to 36 hours from dispatch.

📦

Wednesday–Friday Dispatch

We only dispatch Monday to Wednesday to avoid weekend delays. Orders placed Thursday–Sunday ship the following Monday.

🌍

France & EU Delivery

Currently shipping to France, Belgium, Netherlands, Germany, Switzerland, and the UK. International orders by inquiry.

☑

Arrival Guarantee

If your box arrives in unsatisfactory condition, we will replace it without question. Your first tasting should be perfect.

Each box is hand-assembled on dispatch day, wrapped in kraft paper, and sealed with a wax stamp

Pairing Guide

What to Serve
with Our Cheeses

Every tasting box includes a printed cellar pairing guide — but here are our general principles for building a memorable board.

I

Wine

Burgundy whites (Chardonnay) for alpine. Beaujolais for washed rinds. Late-harvest dessert wines for blue-veined wheels.

II

Bread

Simple, lightly salted sourdough. Avoid flavoured crackers that compete with complex rinds. Walnut bread suits our alpine selection beautifully.

III

Accompaniments

Honeycomb, quince paste, fresh figs, and roasted nuts are all included in our boxes and selected to complement, never overpower.
